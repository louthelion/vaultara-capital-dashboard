Reporting Flow

Monday: Vaultara Capital prepares company reports, funding issues, approvals, financial items, contracts, and updates.

Monday end of day: Vaultara sends the clean package to TitanCore Holdings.

TitanCore Holdings: TitanCore receives, reviews, approves, and prepares the parent-company package.

General Team Review: TitanCore sends the organized package upward for General review.

Mantle Review: Final decisions move through the Mantle of 7 and then back down as company actions.
